Welcome to the Bay Area Sailing Club

Discover the joy of sailing on the beautiful San Francisco Bay! Whether you're an experienced sailor or new to the water, our club offers unforgettable experiences, expert instruction, and a vibrant community of sailing enthusiasts. Join us for day sails, charters, races, and social events.

“It’s remarkable how quickly a good and favorable wind can sweep away the maddening frustrations of shore living.”

Ritz Carlton of charter boats

At Bay Area Sailing Club, we pride ourselves on offering the best charter boats in the Bay Area. Our fleet includes a variety of sailboats, each equipped with modern amenities and safety features to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable sailing experience.

Best instructors

Bay Area Sailing club offers the best qualified US Coast Guard certified captains to provide the best and most affordable training. They undergo rigorous training in all facets of sailing to include navigation, weather predictions, and safety at sea.
