About Us

Bay Area Sailing Club was founded by passionate sailors who wanted to share the thrill and beauty of sailing on the San Francisco Bay. Our mission is to make sailing accessible, safe, and fun for everyone—whether you’re a seasoned sailor or just starting out.

“Sailing is not just a sport, it’s a way of life. Join our community and experience the freedom of the open water.”

Our Vision

To be the leading sailing club in the Bay Area, fostering a vibrant community and promoting lifelong learning and adventure on the water.

Our Values

  • Safety first
  • Inclusivity and community
  • Respect for the environment
  • Excellence in instruction

Meet Our Team

Our team is made up of certified instructors, experienced sailors, and enthusiastic volunteers dedicated to making your sailing experience unforgettable.
